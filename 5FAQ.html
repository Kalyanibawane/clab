Frequently Asked Questions

What is mehndi?

Mehndi is a form of body art that involves applying henna paste to the skin, creating beautiful designs that are temporary and last for several days.

How long does mehndi last?

Typically, mehndi can last from 5 to 15 days depending on the skin type and aftercare.

Is mehndi safe for everyone?

Yes, natural henna is safe for most people. However, it's advisable to do a patch test if you have sensitive skin or allergies.

How do I care for my mehndi?

To prolong the life of your mehndi, avoid scrubbing the area, keep it dry, and avoid water for the first few hours after application.
